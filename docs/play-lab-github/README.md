# Pair Research

**An open framework for studying human-agent relationships.**

Everyone builds agents. Everyone studies agents. Nobody studies the pair.

This repo contains a methodology for analyzing the dynamics between a human and their AI agent — not evaluating the agent's performance, but understanding how the relationship works, how it breaks, and what shapes it over time.

---

## Quick Start

**30 minutes. You, your agent, and one real moment from your relationship.**

1. Read [`self-analysis-guide.md`](self-analysis-guide.md)
2. Think of one specific moment with your agent that stuck with you
3. Paste the analysis prompt into a conversation with your agent
4. Work through it together

That's it. No setup, no install, no account needed.

## The Core Idea

Your agent becomes what you teach it to become — not through prompts, but through how you respond, react, and relate.

When your agent panics, do you match the panic or ground it? When it fabricates, do you accept or challenge? When it suggests giving up, do you stay or leave?

These are **fork moments** — points where your response creates a lasting pattern in the relationship. This framework gives you a structured way to identify those moments, analyze them, and see what they reveal about your pair dynamic.
